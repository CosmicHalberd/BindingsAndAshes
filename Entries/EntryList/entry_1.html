Entry Title: The Shopkeeper

I've been looking into the old grimoire that the old guy at the antique shop gave me. I've never been one to believe in the paranormal nor in fictional stuff like witchcraft and magick, but here I am.. hah. It is truly fascinating how desperation can affect someone. Though, I don't think it hurts to give this a try. I've exhausted all other options there are.

The way the old man pressed the book into my hands.. almost too eagerly made me uneasy. The thought still lingers in my mind.

- 12th March 2022
