Entry Title: Ancient Secrets or Fairy Tales?

You know.. I used to not believe in childish superstitions. But the more i read about the occult the more it seems to make sense in it's own twisted way. I feel as though I'm exploring a new type of science, one that mankind has not explored and define it deeply yet. Of course, I'm not yet convinced if any of these would actually work.

Though I find it interesting that various cultures around the globe sometimes can describe a specific entity with similar traits and sometimes even similar names. Makes you wonder if they saw things that we didn't.
