Entry Title: My First Ritual

The book is quite comprehensive. It has rituals ranging from small charms to more complex and demanding rituals. After reading some more, I tried a ritual that supposedly calls on rain. It required simple materials like dirt, water, and stones. Though on my first attempt nothing seemed to happen. Second attempt, no luck. Third, the same as the first two. Maybe I did something wrong, or maybe it really is just some dumb fairy tale.

I mean, what fool would believe that chanting words while holding a bowl of water would somehow make the weather rain. Though for a moment I felt like someone was watching me, must be just my imagination.

- 22nd March 2022
